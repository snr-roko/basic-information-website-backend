What is Digital Well-being?

Digital well-being refers to the impact of technologies on our mental, physical, and emotional health. This site provides essential tips on maintaining a healthy relationship with technology.

Why it Matters

In an increasingly connected world, balancing our online and offline lives is more important than ever. Proper digital well-being can enhance productivity, reduce stress, and improve overall happiness.
